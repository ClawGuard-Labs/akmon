## Before opening an issue

- Search [existing issues](https://github.com/ClawGuard-Labs/akmon/issues?q=is%3Aissue) — your problem may already be known.
- Run `sudo bash scripts/check_deps.sh` and include its output if the issue is build- or runtime-related.
- Include your kernel version, distro, Go version, and exact `akmon` flags.
- If you can, include a minimal reproducing script.

## Response expectations

Akmon is maintained by volunteers plus the ClawGuard Labs team. We triage new issues within roughly 5 business days. Security reports get priority — see [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for the SLA.

## Commercial support

Commercial or custom-integration inquiries: open a [Discussion](https://github.com/ClawGuard-Labs/akmon/discussions) under the "Announcements" category tagging a maintainer, and we'll reach out.
